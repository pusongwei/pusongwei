Preface
Tony Brown

“Design matters！” 似乎是后疫情时代带着某种坚定的呼唤，危机令我们切身感受到人类生存的困境和背后的隐患，但同时也促使我们回望、复盘：究竟要构建怎样的世界我们才能更接近自然与人类平衡的逆熵状态，“设计”作为当代社会发展与经济建设的重要一环，决定着社会结构中人与人之间的关系如何定义与形成，设计的结果往往成为人类身体的延申，决定着“人”将以怎样的面貌存在，“设计”既是名词也是动词，它承载着人类富有生命力的创造行动形塑日常生活，勾勒着世界未来的图景

设计教育本身作为担负着更重的社会责任感的一种设计形式，引导并实践着如何将对世界的认知、人的需求、解决问题的策略与个体化的表达汇聚成一种多维度的力量从而塑造出创新与技术能力兼备的设计者。艾德艺术设计学院秉承这一理念，将设计行动贯穿整个教育过程，从学科体系既定的专业知识模块构架到课程之外的开放式活动，促使学生们展开纵向与横向的全面成长，为后续走向社会储备能量。

“艾德呈相”作为此次“design matters”呼唤的一个回应，一种镜像，将艾德艺术学院来自视觉传达设计、环境艺术设计、数字媒体设计三个专业方向的具有不同知识背景,但拥有共同美好愿景的学生通过多样化的作品表达呈现出向内的自我探索,编织出艾德年轻设计者的群像,并描绘出对外在世界的多重想象。

“Design Matters!” We are all impacted by the pandemic, with catastrophe revealing the plight and the hidden danger of our existence, which has forced us to look thoughtfully at the balancing between us and nature. As a critical socioeconomic component of today’s world,“design”often defines the construction and structure of interpersonal relationships. With the outcome of the design becoming an extension of our body: effecting human creativity and proactivity, shapes norms today, and is sharping the vision tomorrow.

Emphasizing social responsibility, design education missions is to cultivate creative and skillful designers who can compose aquartet with cosmopolitanism, self-awareness, solution-seeking, and personal expression. Adhering to this philosophy, Eurasia Artand Design (EAAD) makes design a pivotal part throughout its education: from know-how in class to open events to practice,EAAD uses design education to boost the development of its students.

"EAaD Mirroring”is a response to the"Design Matters”call-out, it presents the work of EAaD students majoring in Visual Communication Design, Environmental Art Design, and Digital Media. Students'various background works in concert with their shared vision, and the unison showcases the inner perceptions of EAaD’s forward-looking young designers as well as their reveries gbout the today world.
